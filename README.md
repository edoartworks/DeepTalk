# Deep Talk
is a conversation card game to connect deeper with friends, lovers or strangers.

Deep Talk presents you with a deck of questions, designed to spark meaningful conversations. 

You can choose to customise your deck, by adding or removing levels of Depth. You can keep the conversation light and fun, or decide to go very deep.
You also have the option to add custom questions, or remove questions that you don't like.

You decide how to play.
Ask and answer questions.
Let the conversation flow.
Respect others' boundaries, and your own boundaries.

Have courage.
Be open.
Be vulnerable.
Dare to trust.

---

Features coming in future updates:
* Import multiple questions from a text file
* Tutorial/Onboarding flow
* Support multiple languages
* Screen and card transition animations
